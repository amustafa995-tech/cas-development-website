What Happens Next

A response within 2 business days

Our team reviews every inquiry personally. After your first message, expect a scoping call to understand your product, your target market, and the regulatory pathway. We guide you through the AMM process, local distribution, and hospital/pharmacy rollout.

Based in Geneva (CET/CEST) and Baghdad (AST), we typically respond within one business day — never later than two.

1. Initial Contact Share your product portfolio and target territory.
2. Scoping Call 30-minute discovery to align on regulatory and commercial fit.
3. Proposal & NDA Tailored plan covering AMM, distribution logistics, and partnership terms.
4. Go-to-Market Coordinated rollout across our Iraqi distribution network.
